Solid Ground has many year round events. From weekend retreats to day long events, there are tons of opportunities for fellowship and fun. Hope you can come!

  • SNAC

    (SNAC) Sunday Night After Church
    is a once a month event where
    students gather at someone's
    house after the evening service for
    fellowship and food.

    Learn More

  • Senior Outing

    A senior outing to form
    friendships that lead and
    give life.

    Sept. 19

  • Dunes Day

    A day spent at Sleeping Bear Dunes.
    A remote(ish) beach,
    frisbee, bocce ball, swimming
    and most likely a bit of dune jumping.
    Bring money for dinner and shopping.

    Sep. 26

  • Fall Retreat

    A weekend at Camp Ao-Wa-Kiya for a time of spiritual growth as well as
    many fun games and challenges.
    Warning- night games may be too
    intense for the faint of heart!

    Nov. 6-8

  • Movie Day

    This year we plan to have a day-long Star Wars movie marathon. We will watch all or most of the complete saga and then
    follow up with watching Star Wars Episode 7
    in theaters.

    Learn More

  • Amore

    The big youth group fundraiser
    for supplying funds for the rest of
    the year. Each year there is a different theme.
    Students set up, serve food, act,
    sing, and clean up.

    Learn More

  • Spring Fling

    A youth group trip to Chicago. For a day full of downtown fun and fellowship.

    Learn More

  • Rainy River

    A mission trip to Morson, Ontario, helping out at a week long VBS hosted by the Mast and Weaver families.
